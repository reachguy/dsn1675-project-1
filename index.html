ABOUT ME

My name is Shogo, and I am a Graphic Designer based in Ottawa, ON. I have a strong passion for skateboarding, photography, and anything that is creative and artistic.

I recently started working for a skateboard company called Moodra Skateboards, and hope to share my passion with the youth of Ottawa, so that they may find their voice and express themselves the same way that skateboarding has helped me express myself. In addition to that, I am working as the In-House Graphic Designer at Red Diamond Screen Printing.

I am also actively involved in the Ottawa skateboard community, and volunteer for various fundraisers with For Pivots Sake , which is a non-profit organization founded by Antique Skate Shop to engage and mentor less fortunate youth in the Ottawa community.
